Steps to publish new Version to nextcloud (only for admin)

Sign code:
sudo docker compose exec -u www-data nextcloud php occ integrity:sign-app 
          --privateKey /var/www/html/apps-shared/timesheet.key
          --certificate /var/www/html/apps-shared/timesheet.crt
          --path /var/www/html/apps-shared/timesheet

Zip Project:
tar -czf timesheet-0.0.0.tar.gz --exclude-vcs timesheet/

Appstore sign:
sudo openssl dgst -sha512 -sign timesheet.key timesheet-0.0.0.tar.gz | openssl base64
